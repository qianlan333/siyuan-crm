## Safety Notes

- Every route in this manifest is `GET` and readonly.
- Sample-dependent routes cannot be `yes_full` until a safe old test service returns a representative `external_userid`.
- Missing sample data should be recorded as skipped/pending-sample, not silently treated as full gray evidence.
- 2026-05-20 local masked sample coverage exists for detail/timeline/recent-message shape checks, but production data backfill and route cutover remain out of scope.
- Real WeCom contact sync, message archive sync, tag refresh, OpenClaw webhook, and production data backfill are out of scope.
- Rollback always points to old Flask route ownership until production cutover is approved.
